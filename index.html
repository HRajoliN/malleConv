About

Hi, I am Yifan Jiang (江亦凡), a second-year Ph.D student at University of Texas at Austin, VITA Group, supervised by Prof. Zhangyang Wang. Before that I spent one year at Texas A&M University. I received my bachelor degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology, Wuhan, China. My interests include Deep Learning and Computer Vision. Now I mainly work on the area of Generative Models, Neural Architecture Search, and High-level Representation Learning.
